Content & Purpose

Tour de Niu is my personal travel journal. In 2020, between the first two COVID-19 waves in Europe, I bought a used eletric scooter and did a tour through France. I want to share my experiences with my friends, family and the occasional stranger on the internet.
Also I want to show a way to do low impact environmentally friendly tourism and encourage others to make their own dream tours. I hope you all enjoy what I post. If you have feedback or questions, please write me a mail or talk to me on twitter.
